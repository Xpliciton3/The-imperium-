# Document-driven features added or locked by analysis

These features were pulled from the uploaded Imperium documents and then encoded into the app bundle:

- **Pre-Rite vs Post-Rite mode**
  - derived from rites-of-passage material and readiness logic
- **Rite timestamp anchor**
  - date/time stored because the rite becomes an important historical threshold
- **Readiness engine**
  - doctrine, oath, litany, Vel'nar foundations, warrior foundations, meal/grocery planning, routine consistency, and rite understanding
- **Today spine / guided daily walkthrough**
  - the app tells the user exactly what to do next instead of acting like a loose library
- **Doctrine quick access**
  - oath, litany, emergency litany, bookmarks, recent doctrine
- **Holy day expansion**
  - mood, meaning, preparation, primary rite, and four lawful ways to observe
- **Meal-plan grocery builder**
  - pantry staples, batch-cooking logic, ingredient-level add buttons for store and online lists
- **Glossary / tap-to-define layer**
  - specialized language and warrior terms explained in plain English with deeper notes
- **Vel'nar tutor shell**
  - lesson progression, tests, score slot, and room for canonical audio later
- **Warrior progression architecture**
  - beginner / intermediate / advanced structure with safety and mistakes implied in the definitions layer
